# 🏘️ NearBy – A Productive Neighbourhood

A smart and centralized platform to connect, communicate, and collaborate within your neighbourhood.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

NearBy is a web-based neighbourhood communication and marketplace platform designed to replace fragmented communication methods like WhatsApp groups, paper notices, and word-of-mouth sharing.

It provides a **secure, structured, and verified digital environment** where residents can stay informed, interact, and trade locally. 

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Traditional neighbourhood communication suffers from:

* Unorganized information sharing
* Missed important announcements
* Lack of a trusted centralized platform
* Poor emergency communication
* Limited local commerce and service sharing

These gaps reduce community engagement and efficiency. 

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Eliminate communication gaps with a structured digital notice board
* Ensure security through verified user authentication
* Automate administrative tasks for community managers
* Enable resource sharing (skills, services, goods)
* Provide real-time emergency alert system 

---

## 💡 Solution

NearBy provides:

* 🧩 **Centralized Digital Hub** – One platform for all community activities
* 🔐 **Role-Based Access Control** – Secure login for residents & admins
* 🔔 **Real-Time Notifications** – Instant alerts for emergencies and updates
* 🗂️ **Structured Categories** – Events, Maintenance, Emergency, Marketplace
* 🤝 **Community Engagement Tools** – Comments, polls, help requests

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 User Features (Residents)

* User registration & login
* Profile management
* Community posts (Events, Lost & Found, Announcements, Donations)
* Event calendar
* Privacy settings
* Real-time notifications 

---

### 🛒 Marketplace Features

#### 🧑‍💼 Buyer

* Buy products/services from neighbours
* Online & offline payment options
* Order tracking & notifications
* Delivery or pickup selection 

#### 🏪 Seller

* Product listing & inventory management
* Customer chat system
* Order tracking
* Reviews & ratings
* Promotions & advertisements 

#### 🚴 Rider

* Delivery dashboard
* Order list management
* Complaint system
* Support helpline 

---

### 🛠️ Admin Features

* Resident verification
* Notice management
* Content moderation
* Dashboard for community control

---

### 🚨 Emergency System

* Priority-based alert system
* Instant notifications for critical events (fire, theft, medical)
* Highlighted emergency posts
